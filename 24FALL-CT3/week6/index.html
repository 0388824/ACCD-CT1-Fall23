Melting Glacier Simulation Documentation

Project Overview

This simulation illustrates the melting of glaciers to raise awareness of climate change and its impact on human life. By moving away from Unity's default look, this project emphasizes a unique, immersive experience that contrasts natural and human-made environments, showing how the melting process threatens societal stability and cultural heritage.

Visual Representation and Design Choices

The design emphasizes a cold, isolating atmosphere with custom textures and lighting to create a distinct environment. Elements are carefully chosen to represent both the literal and figurative impact of glacier melting. The glacier’s collapse symbolizes environmental degradation, while rising water overtaking structures represents cultural and societal loss.

Melting Simulation Video

This video captures the full melting process, illustrating the glacier’s transformation and the impact on surrounding structures as water levels rise, symbolizing the environmental consequences of climate change.

Making Process and Technical Choices

This project required calibration of the melting effect to balance realism with visual impact. Custom shaders and lighting adjustments were made to create a convincing, immersive experience. Iterations focused on refining the glacier’s behavior and water rising to achieve a compelling transition from stability to collapse.

Challenges: Managing performance and particle effects for realistic melting behavior, along with lighting transitions, were crucial to maintaining visual immersion and impact.

Used loop cuts to mimic the glacier collapsing in stages, creating a realistic fragmentation effect as melting progressed.

Applied the Aniall plugin to animate vertices, creating a gradual melting effect that visually conveyed the degradation of ice.

Edited the water shader to include waves, enhanced with HDR to create a realistic, immersive water surface that reflects the melting scene.

Reflection and Final Thoughts

This simulation is designed to provoke reflection on the immediate and long-term impacts of glacier melting. By presenting the melting process in stages, the project highlights how environmental changes unfold and affect human life, encouraging viewers to consider the consequences of inaction on climate issues.
